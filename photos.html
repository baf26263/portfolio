Work Crew

A huge part of YL is camp, and a huge part of running a camp is staff. With YL being a non-profit, volunteers are very necessary. So what they do, is split every summer into 3 "sessions", and each session, a new group of volunteers come to run the camp, typically one for June, July, and August. In August 2016 my senior summer I did the High school version called Work Crew. These are just a few of the people I did it with, and are friendships I still keep up with to this day.
